OUR VISION

  • In a world where technology brings about a divide, Village would like to challenge that norm. We want to create a sustainable and reliable network for immigrants who feel alienated in an unknown community. Village is the avenue that unites us.

    • HOW IT WORKS

  • After registering and taking our survey, we match you with people who speak a similar language and have shared interests. We give you multiple options in which you can choose from. After you choose your match, you can meet up with your new friend!
    1. Register and Take Our Survey
    2. Wait for us to match you.
    3. Meet and connect with your new friend!
